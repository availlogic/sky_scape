Controller Calibration

Connect your FPV transmitter or USB gamepad, then click Start to begin calibration.

Step 1: Throttle

Move your throttle stick (usually left stick vertical) all the way UP, then click Next.

Step 2: Yaw

Move your yaw stick (usually left stick horizontal) all the way LEFT, then click Next.

Step 3: Pitch

Move your pitch stick (usually right stick vertical) all the way UP, then click Next.

Step 4: Roll

Move your roll stick (usually right stick horizontal) all the way RIGHT, then click Next.

Step 5: Verification

Verify stick movements. The dot should follow your sticks correctly.

Controller Disconnected

Reconnect controller or press Escape to switch to Keyboard mode.
